Which Social Media Platform is Hurting You The Most?

It's not a secret that our generation is brutally addicted to our phones. Whether that be scrolling through TikTok for hours, refreshing our Instagram feeds, or actively sending selfies back and forth on Snapchat, we cannot seem to escape the mental health prison that social media has locked us in. As an avid user of these three platforms, I've come to recognize the harmful effects placed on my mental health, such as anxiety, stress, and body-image issues that I can't seem to escape. With this being said, do you ever wonder which platform might be the most toxic for your mental health, and can we change the way we view these apps through our own self-awareness?
